How It Works

BullyPool aims to reduce the stress on Starkville's crumbling infastructure by giving students a mean to connect and plan out ridesharing, in the hopes of reducing our carbon footprint while also giving commuters some much needed room to breathe.

Users of BullyPool will be able to communicate using Travel Request Forms (TRF's). These TRF's (pictured below/above/left/right) will the user's location from a list of supported apartments, as well the user's time of departure and their desired time of arrival.
